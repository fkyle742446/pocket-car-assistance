Pocket Car Assistance Center

Welcome to the Pocket Car Assistance Center! Find answers, guides, and resources to help you enjoy Pocket Car to the fullest.

Getting Started

  • How to Download and Install: Visit the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android), search for Pocket Car, and install it.
  • Creating and Managing Your Account: Open the app, register, and set up your profile.
  • Daily Booster Packs: Receive two free packs daily to grow your collection!

Features Guide

  • Opening Booster Packs: Go to the "Packs" section and tap to reveal your cards.
  • Managing Your Collection: Organize your cards in binders and create custom display boards.
  • Immersive Cards: Discover cards with stunning 3D-inspired illustrations.
